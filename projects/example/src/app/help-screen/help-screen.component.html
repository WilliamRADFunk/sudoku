SuDoKu ExTrEme
Each quadrant must have numbers 1 through 9 without duplicates.

There can be no duplicate numbers in any row or column.

Every SuDoKu board has exactly one correct combination of numbers, and all spaces must be filled to complete a board.

When all boards are complete...you win!

There are 2 modes for interacting with a SuDoKu board.

Answer Mode is the pencil icon (activated by clicking flag button) and any number highlighted will be the number you enter into a cell when you click there.

There other mode for interacting with a SuDoKu board is Flag Mode.

Flag Mode is the button with a flag icon (activated by clicking pencil button) and any number highlighted will be the number you enter into a cell when you click there.

This has no effect on the outcome of a game, but can help you keep track of your options.

Each level of Sudoku Extreme increases the number of boards in play exponentially: 1, 9, 81, 729, 6561, ...

Build time increases significantly for each level chosen as all boards are interconnected.

With the exception of the highest level,
each board's quadrant is associated with an entire sudoku board in the level above it.

Since there are 9 quadrants to a board, each board has 9 additional boards associated with it in the level above.
